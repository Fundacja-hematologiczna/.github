## About
The Foundation for Helping People with Leukemia is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting patients diagnosed with leukemia and other blood cancers. We focus on providing medical, psychological, and financial assistance to those in need, as well as promoting research, education, and public awareness about blood diseases.
